    Dreame L10s Ultra vs Roborock Qrevo S

    Both flagship-tier combo vacuum-mops with self-cleaning bases at the same price tier. The decision comes down to home layout, pet situation, and which ecosystem you trust.

    Sources analyzed: RTINGS Slickdeals owner discussions Reddit (r/robotvacuums) Notebookcheck · How we score →

    Quick Verdict

    Choose Dreame L10s Ultra if…

    your home has lots of small objects or toys to navigate around, and you want best-in-class edge-mopping with MopExtend technology.

    Choose Roborock Qrevo S if…

    you have pets, want faster cleaning cycles, and prefer Roborock's more mature app + larger user community for troubleshooting.

    What Each Does Best

    Dreame L10s Ultra

    • MopExtend edge-mopping — extending mop reaches into corners and along walls better than the Qrevo S's standard mop pattern. Slickdeals owner ekguru specifically cited this as the L10s's deciding advantage. Source: Slickdeals owner comparison, Notebookcheck
    • Superior obstacle avoidance in cluttered homes — the L10s identifies and navigates around small objects (toys, cables) more reliably than the Qrevo S. Source: Slickdeals direct comparison
    • Hot air drying in base — reduces mop pad mildew and odor between cleaning cycles. Source: Notebookcheck, Dreame spec
    • Roboswing + dual rotating mops — actively scrubs rather than dragging, more effective on dried stains than the Qrevo S's mop pattern. Source: RTINGS testing

    Roborock Qrevo S

    • Full-rubber main brush — significantly better for pet hair — Slickdeals owner ekguru specifically called out the Qrevo S as the better choice for pet homes because the rubber brush resists tangles, where the Dreame requires a separate "TriCut" brush accessory. Source: Slickdeals owner consensus
    • Faster cleaning cycles — RTINGS specifically noted the Qrevo S clean times are shorter than the L10s for comparable floor areas. Source: RTINGS comparison
    • Larger 2.5L dirtbag — vs the Dreame's 2.15L; fewer bag changes over time. Source: RTINGS
    • Longer battery life — RTINGS rated Qrevo S battery life as "considerably longer" than the L10s. Source: RTINGS
    • Mature app and larger user community — Roborock's app has had more iterations and Reddit has more troubleshooting threads, firmware tips, and community workarounds. Source: r/robotvacuums consensus

    The Real Trade-offs

    Dreame L10s Ultra downsides

    • Hair tangle management requires separate accessory — the bundled brush tangles easier than Roborock's rubber design; need the TriCut attachment for pet homes. Source: Slickdeals owner discussion
    • Newer platform, smaller user community — fewer Reddit threads when troubleshooting niche issues. Source: r/robotvacuums activity comparison
    • Some owners report onboard dust bin jamming — Slickdeals owner JasonP15 (two L10s units, long-term) reports occasional debris jams requiring manual clearing. Source: Slickdeals long-term owner

    Roborock Qrevo S downsides

    • Mopping pattern less effective on corners and edges — without MopExtend equivalent, the Qrevo S misses the same edges every cleaning pass. Source: Notebookcheck Roborock vs Dreame analysis
    • No FlexiArm equivalent on the standard Qrevo S — that feature is only on the more expensive Qrevo MaxV. Source: Notebookcheck

    Our Recommendation

    Genuinely close, and the right answer depends on your home. For pet homes, the Roborock Qrevo S is the clearer pick — the rubber brush is meaningfully better for hair, and that's an everyday quality-of-life difference. For cluttered homes with kids' toys, multiple rooms with awkward layouts, or hard floors that need genuinely thorough edge-mopping, the Dreame L10s Ultra edges ahead. If you're risk-averse or want a more established support community, default to Roborock.
