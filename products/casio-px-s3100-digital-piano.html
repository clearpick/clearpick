    ✅ What Works

    • Smart Scaled Hammer Action keys vary weight across the 88-key range — heavier in the bass, lighter in the treble, mirroring acoustic piano key behavior. This is a significant step up from non-weighted or uniformly-weighted digital piano keys, and helps develop proper playing technique.
    • Multi-Dimensional Morphing AiR sound engine samples multiple velocity layers — the piano sound changes naturally across the dynamic range (pp through fff) because Casio sampled multiple layers and morphs between them based on how hard keys are struck.
    • Bluetooth audio and MIDI enables wireless connection to apps — the PX-S3100 connects wirelessly to the Casio app for lesson content, and streams audio wirelessly to Bluetooth speakers. The wireless MIDI also works with DAWs and iOS piano apps.
    • 232mm depth makes it the slimmest 88-key piano available — the PX-S series body is shallower than any competitor at this key count, allowing placement in rooms where a traditional digital piano or stage piano wouldn't physically fit.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • No ivory-feel keys at this price — the PX-S3100 keys have a smooth plastic surface, not the textured ivory-feel coating of higher-end Casio, Roland, or Yamaha models. For players transitioning from acoustic piano, the key surface texture may feel slipperier than expected.
    • 700 tones include many non-piano sounds of limited utility — the tones include orchestra, synth, and GM sounds most piano players won't use regularly. The piano, EP, and organ tones are the ones most worth evaluating before purchase.
    • The 4-speaker system is adequate but not room-filling — at 8+4W (24W total), the PX-S3100's built-in speakers are among the better ones in this price tier, but won't fill a medium room at high volumes. External speakers or headphones improve the experience.
    • Stand sold separately — the PX-S3100 is a slab-style digital piano that requires purchasing an X-stand or dedicated CS-92 Casio stand separately (typically $50-150 CAD additional).
