    ✅ What Works

    • Integrates with the RYOBI 40V ecosystem — buyers who own the RYOBI 40V HP Brushless mower or 40V blower share batteries across tools without extra chargers or purchase cost.
    • At ~6.7 lb with battery, it's slightly lighter than the EGO ST1521S — a small but noticeable difference during extended edge trimming along long fence lines or driveways.
    • Variable speed trigger gives control for different tasks — slow for careful trimming near garden beds where flying debris could damage plants, fast for open overgrowth along fences.
    • 15" cutting swath handles typical residential edging and trimming at a pace appropriate for urban and suburban Canadian lots.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • RYOBI 40V and RYOBI 18V ONE+ batteries are physically different and not interchangeable. This is one of the most common buyer errors — many RYOBI 18V users assume compatibility and are disappointed. Confirm you're buying 40V.
    • .080" line diameter is lighter than EGO's .095" — it handles standard grass and light weeds well but will struggle with thicker brush or heavy overgrowth. Line replacement spools in .080" are widely available.
    • Battery inclusion varies by listing — tool-only models are common. Existing 40V users get excellent value; new buyers must budget for a 40V battery and charger separately.
    • Not compatible with RYOBI attachment accessories the way EGO's split shaft is — this is a straight shaft trimmer with no attachment expansion option.
