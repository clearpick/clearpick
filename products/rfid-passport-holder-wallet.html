    ✅ What Works

    • 12 card slots accommodate your full travel card set—passport, boarding passes, multiple credit cards, insurance cards, hotel cards, and transit passes—in one organized document that opens to give everything in one view at airport check-in
    • RFID-blocking lining throughout the wallet protects both passport chip and credit card chips from electronic skimming while the wallet is closed—a genuine security benefit in crowded transit hubs and tourist areas
    • The SIM card pocket is a small but thoughtful detail that frequent international travelers who swap SIM cards for local data find invaluable—a dedicated home for SIMs prevents losing them in pockets or zip pouches
    • PU leather exterior holds its shape and feels premium in hand at a price that's half of genuine leather alternatives; it survives airport security belt drops, bag throws, and pocket sweat without visible damage for 2–3 years of heavy use

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • PU leather eventually cracks and peels at stress points (zipper edges, corner folds) after 3–4 years of daily heavy travel use—genuine leather alternatives last longer for the frequent traveler
    • The RFID blocking on passport chips is technically effective but also arguably unnecessary—contactless passport chip skimming in the wild is extremely rare compared to how it's marketed; card skimming protection is the more practically relevant feature
    • 12 cards fill the wallet to a firm thickness that is noticeable in a jacket breast pocket; neck pouch wear or a slim front-pocket carry is more practical than trying to fit this into a pants pocket
