    ✅ What Works

    • LG's QNED80 combines NanoCell color filtering with quantum dots for wide color gamut coverage — skin tones, natural colors, and saturated content like animation and sport graphics are all handled accurately and vividly. LG's color science is among the best in the industry.
    • webOS 24 remains one of the most polished smart TV platforms — the Magic Remote with point-and-click cursor control makes navigation genuinely fast, the interface is clean and uncluttered, and LG's app support is comprehensive including all major Canadian streaming services.
    • True 120Hz native refresh rate with G-Sync and FreeSync Premium makes this a legitimately excellent gaming TV. Input lag in Game Optimizer mode drops to under 5ms, VRR runs smooth, and ALLM switches to game mode automatically when a console is detected.
    • LG's Dolby Vision IQ implementation is one of the best available — it reads ambient light via built-in sensor and adjusts tone mapping continuously, so the picture looks correct whether you're watching at noon in a sunlit room or at midnight with everything dark.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • NanoCell panels have limited local dimming compared to Mini-LED TVs — blacks in dark scenes aren't as deep as TCL's Q6 Mini-LED at a lower price. LG wins on color accuracy and gaming features but loses on pure contrast performance.
    • At $1,099 CAD, the QNED80 is priced significantly above the TCL Q6 and Hisense U8N — both of which offer Mini-LED panels with potentially better contrast for less money. LG's advantages are webOS, gaming features, and build quality, not peak picture performance.
    • The Magic Remote is excellent but the LR1000 replacement remote sold separately is expensive if lost or broken. LG's ThinQ app can be used as a backup but the experience is inferior to the physical remote.
    • webOS collects viewing data for ad targeting by default — users should opt out during initial setup or in settings. The option is available but buried, and the default-on setting is worth being aware of.
