    ✅ What Works

    • Reframe after shooting is the transformative feature — shoot all 360° at once, then choose your framing in the Insta360 app after the fact. This eliminates missed shots from wrong camera angles and allows cinematic framing decisions in post that would be impossible to anticipate while filming.
    • AI-powered editing app turns raw 360° footage into ready-to-post clips with minimal manual work — the app can detect highlights, apply stabilization, choose frames, and add music automatically. This is genuinely faster than traditional editing for social content.
    • Invisible selfie stick works natively — because the stick is within the camera's field of view on both sensors, the stitching algorithm removes it automatically. The result is footage that appears to float in mid-air or be filmed by nothing.
    • 135-minute battery is class-leading for action cameras — nearly 3x a GoPro HERO13 per charge, enabling full-day adventure recording without carrying spare batteries.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • 8K 360° footage produces enormous file sizes — approximately 300MB per minute, meaning a 256GB card holds roughly 14 hours of 8K footage. Plan storage accordingly.
    • Rendering 360° footage is CPU-intensive — editing on a laptop from 2019 or older may be slow; the app handles processing on the device or in the cloud, but exporting finished clips can take time.
    • Below 10m requires a separate waterproof housing — the IPX8 rating covers to 10m without housing, but for scuba or deeper water sports, the Dive Case accessory is necessary.
    • The 360° workflow is different from traditional cameras — if you prefer traditional viewfinder/LCD composition, the post-capture reframing workflow requires a mindset shift.
