    ✅ What Works

    • Deepest iPhone integration of any smartwatch — calls, texts, notifications, Siri, Apple Pay, and Find My all work natively without opening an app or unlocking your phone.
    • ECG and blood oxygen monitoring are health-grade features at consumer prices — Apple Watch has detected atrial fibrillation and low blood oxygen in users who had no symptoms, potentially saving lives.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Only works with iPhone — the Apple Watch is completely dependent on an iPhone; no Android compatibility whatsoever.
    • 18-hour battery requires nightly charging — the always-on display and comprehensive health monitoring drain the battery enough to require nightly charging, limiting sleep tracking convenience.
