    ✅ What Works

    • TEMPUR material provides the best pressure relief available in any mattress — NASA-derived visco-elastic foam distributes weight unlike any other material, making it the top recommendation for chronic pain sufferers.
    • Motion isolation is absolute — partners don't feel each other move at all on Tempur-Pedic. The wine glass test (wine glass on the mattress while partner moves) is a Tempur-Pedic trademark.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Very expensive at $3,499 CAD — almost double the Saatva Classic for a shorter trial period. Only justified for sleepers who've tried everything else and need maximum pressure relief.
    • Sleeps warm — TEMPUR material is denser than standard foam and retains more body heat. Hot sleepers should look at the Breeze version or the Purple Rejuvenate Plus instead.
