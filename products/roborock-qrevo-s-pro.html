    ✅ What Works

    • The 75°C hot-water mop wash is the most-cited upgrade over cheaper dock models — owners note grease, pet-oil residue, and kitchen-floor grime clear more effectively than with cold-water competitors. This is the feature that separates the S Pro tier from the QV 35A.
    • 18,500Pa suction handles hard floors convincingly. Oatmeal, rice, coffee beans, and loose debris come up in reviews as single-pass cleanup without requiring multiple runs.
    • The all-rubber anti-tangle brush gets consistent credit in Reddit threads — long hair and pet fur are guided to brush ends rather than wrapping, eliminating the most common maintenance task on older robot vacuums.
    • Roborock's app is cited by nearly every reviewer as the most polished in the category — SmartPlan 2.0, per-room settings, and Thorough+ mode are practical differentiators owners actually use daily.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Side brush cannot extend past the robot body — a consistent note in detailed reviews — leaving a strip along baseboards and furniture legs that requires manual attention. The Qrevo Curv's FlexiArm extension solves this.
    • On higher-pile carpet, suction numbers don't fully translate to pickup depth. Debris residue on medium-to-high pile is a real-world limitation despite the 18,500Pa rating.
    • Price can fluctuate on Amazon.ca — verify current pricing before purchase. Confirmed at approximately $749.99 CAD from Roborock CA promotional pricing as of research date.

    Common complaints

    Corner and edge cleaning comes up…

    Corner and edge cleaning comes up repeatedly as a gap — neither the side brush nor the mop modules extend beyond the robot's footprint, and owners in detailed reviews note that baseboards and furniture edges require follow-up hand-cleaning.

    Source: Reddit r/RobotVacuums

    Obstacle detection with Reactive Tech struggles…

    Obstacle detection with Reactive Tech struggles with flat or small obstacles — thin cables were run over in hands-on testing, and owners consistently note that dedicated no-go zones or pre-cleaning is needed to avoid this.

    Source: basic-tutorials.com review

    High-pile carpet performance is a recurring…

    High-pile carpet performance is a recurring point of caution — reviewers who tested on taller pile found noticeably more residue than on hard surfaces, and some owners raise it as a reason the Qrevo Curv may be worth the step-up.

    Source: Reddit r/RobotVacuums
    ClearPick Verdict

    Owners who run a hard-floor-heavy home and want a fully automated, hygienically clean dock without flagship pricing consistently land on the Qrevo S Pro as their recommendation. The 75°C mop wash, anti-tangle brush, and polished app are the three features that come up most in real owner write-ups. The step-up to the Qrevo Curv gets recommended when owners have significant carpet, high pet traffic, or want edge-cleaning coverage.
