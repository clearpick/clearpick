    ✅ What Works

    • Dyson's V9 motor spins at 110,000 RPM — nine times faster than typical hair dryer motors — and fits inside the handle rather than the barrel. The handle placement shifts the center of gravity, making the dryer balance differently in the hand with less wrist strain during long drying sessions. For daily users with long hair, this ergonomic difference is meaningful.
    • The glass bead thermistor measures air temperature 40 times per second and adjusts to prevent heat spikes. Conventional dryers output a fixed wattage regardless of proximity to hair, creating temperature variation that causes cumulative damage over time. The Supersonic's precision is genuinely protective for color-treated and fine hair.
    • 13 temperature settings give you granular control over heat exposure. Most hair dryers offer 3 settings where the difference between 'hot' and 'medium' is 50°C. The Supersonic's 13 steps let you dial in exactly what your hair type needs, including a dedicated 'cold' temperature for setting styles without heat.
    • The included flyaway smoother attachment is the HD15's standout addition — it uses curved air channels to smooth surface flyaways without direct contact, replacing what typically requires a round brush while giving results that look more polished than any consumer dryer under $400.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • $699 CAD for a hair dryer is objectively a luxury purchase. The Shark HyperAir at $229 CAD and the Conair InfinitiPRO at $49 CAD both dry hair effectively. The Supersonic's premium is for precision heat technology, motor balance, specific attachment ecosystem, and long-term reliability — not raw drying speed.
    • The Supersonic is quieter than most salon dryers but not silent. Marketing language about being quieter can set unrealistic expectations — it sounds like a normal powerful dryer, just without the high-pitched motor whine. In absolute terms it's still a hair dryer.
    • The magnetic attachments are the most convenient system in the category — they click on and off instantly and never wobble. But they are Dyson-proprietary and won't work with other brands. If you already own Airwrap attachments, some are cross-compatible but not all.
    • Dyson's support and warranty in Canada is generally well-regarded, but repair costs outside warranty are high due to the specialized motor. Most users find it's worth buying a 2-year protection plan if one is available.

    ClearPick Verdict

    The Dyson Supersonic HD15 is the best hair dryer for anyone serious about long-term hair health — its precision heat control demonstrably reduces temperature damage compared to conventional dryers, and the handle-balanced motor makes extended styling sessions more comfortable. For daily users with fine, color-treated, or heat-damaged hair, it's a worthwhile investment.
