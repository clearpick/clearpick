    ✅ What Works

    • Roku OS is the most neutral and user-friendly streaming interface available — cross-app search finds the cheapest way to watch any title across all your subscriptions without bias toward any particular service. The search results tell you if something is free on Pluto TV, available on your Netflix plan, or pay-per-view on Apple TV+.
    • At $69 CAD, the Roku Streaming Stick 4K offers 4K HDR, Dolby Vision, and HDR10+ at a price that undercuts virtually every competitor. For buyers who just want to upgrade an old TV or add 4K streaming to a bedroom set, the value is exceptional.
    • The voice remote's private listening headphone jack is a genuinely thoughtful feature — plug in any headphones and audio plays through them instead of the TV speakers, without pairing Bluetooth or disturbing others in the room. Useful for late-night watching or apartments with thin walls.
    • Long-range Wi-Fi antenna is real and measurable — Roku specifically designed the Streaming Stick 4K to maintain signal in rooms where cheaper streaming sticks fail. Buyers with routers on different floors or in the opposite corner of the house report more reliable connections than competing stick devices.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • The Roku Streaming Stick 4K runs warm during extended 4K streaming — not hot enough to cause problems, but it gets noticeably warm to the touch. Leaving it plugged directly into a TV in a cramped media cabinet with poor airflow has caused throttling for some users.
    • Roku OS doesn't support Dolby Atmos audio passthrough to the same degree as Apple TV or NVIDIA Shield — Atmos is available on some apps (Netflix, Disney+) but not universally across all Roku channels. Buyers with high-end audio systems should verify compatibility with their specific streaming services.
    • The included remote doesn't have backlit buttons — a persistent complaint and one area where the Google TV Streamer and Fire TV Cube remote designs are superior. Roku sells an upgraded 'Voice Remote Pro' separately with a backlit, rechargeable remote if this is important.
    • Roku's free streaming channel (The Roku Channel) includes ads, which can't be fully disabled. The platform is ad-supported in part, which is how they keep device prices low — this is a reasonable trade-off but worth knowing upfront.
