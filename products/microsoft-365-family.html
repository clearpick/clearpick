What Buyers Say

✅ What Works

  • Covers up to 6 users with full Office suite access on all their devices — the most cost-effective Microsoft 365 option for Canadian families
  • Each user gets 1TB of OneDrive storage — 6 total terabytes of family cloud storage for documents, photos, and backups across the whole household
  • Each user gets their own independent account and storage — family members have separate, private storage rather than sharing a single account
  • Microsoft Family Safety features included — parental controls, screen time management, and location sharing for Canadian families with children

⚠️ Worth Knowing

  • Requires sharing the subscription invitation with up to 5 additional users — family members need Microsoft accounts to accept and access their licenses
  • Cost savings over individual plans only materialize when multiple users actively use it — worth calculating actual per-user cost if fewer than 3 family members will use it regularly
  • Better for Canadian families where 3-6 people need regular Office access than for households where only one person uses Office regularly

ClearPick Verdict

Microsoft 365 Family is the right choice for Canadian households where multiple family members need Office and cloud storage. At 6 licenses with 1TB each, the per-person cost makes it significantly more economical than multiple Personal subscriptions. The independent storage accounts and Family Safety features add meaningful value for Canadian families with children. If only one person in your household needs Office, the Personal plan is simpler and less expensive.
