    ✅ What Works

    • 5 amp characters cover the major tonal territory — Clean (Fender-style), Crunch (early Marshall-style), Lead (high-gain), Brown (EVH-style), and Acoustic simulator let the Katana cover everything from country to metal without switching amps.
    • 60+ built-in effects including studio-grade reverbs and delays — the Katana MkII ships with Boss's entire library of effects accessible via the free Tone Studio app. Reverb, delay, chorus, flanger, phaser, and OD/distortions are all included at the quality level of Boss's standalone pedals.
    • Power attenuation (0.5W / 25W / 50W) allows bedroom-friendly use at real amp tone — at 0.5W, the 12-inch speaker runs quiet enough for apartment practice while maintaining the tonal character of tube-inspired solid-state saturation that makes cranked amps sound good.
    • USB recording output to DAW with cab simulation — connect via USB and the Katana appears as an audio interface with cab-simulated output. Record guitar directly to GarageBand, Ableton, or any DAW without additional hardware.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Solid-state, not tube — the Katana uses analog circuitry to simulate tube amp characteristics. Players who have played tube amps for decades may notice a slight difference in response and feel. For most players, including many professionals, the Katana is indistinguishable in live settings.
    • The 12-inch speaker doesn't match the quality of standalone Boss/Waza speakers in premium cabinets — the included speaker is competent, but players who want premium tone at full volume may eventually want an external cab.
    • Effects programming requires the Tone Studio app for full access — the front panel controls three of 60 effects at once. Accessing the full library requires connecting to the Tone Studio app (free, works well). Live players should set up presets before a gig.
    • 14kg is the heaviest amp at this price tier — significantly heavier than small practice amps. The weight is due to the 12-inch speaker and real transformer. Consider a carry case or wheels for regular transport.
