    ✅ What Works

    • MagLatch magnetic closure is the most innovative camera bag feature available — one-handed open and close while the bag is on your back. Traditional buckle closures require two hands; the MagLatch can be opened with a single thumb stroke in motion, making it faster than any other bag on the market for quick camera access.
    • FlexFold dividers transform the bag between camera carry and everyday carry in under 30 seconds — fold them flat and the interior becomes a clean travel bag; unfold them and they create padded compartments for camera bodies and lenses. No other bag makes this transition as cleanly.
    • Build quality is exceptional and backed by a lifetime guarantee — the 400D nylon is thick and abrasion-resistant, zippers are YKK AquaGuard water-resistant, and Peak Design covers defects for the life of the product with no time limit.
    • Side zip access provides reach-in camera retrieval while wearing — not as fast as the MagLatch main opening but provides quick lens or accessory access from the side without removing the bag.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • $319 CAD is premium pricing — the Peak Design is the most expensive 20L camera bag we list, roughly 2x the Lowepro ProTactic BP 350 per litre of camera capacity. The quality and design justify the premium for daily users, but it's not budget-friendly.
    • 13-inch laptop sleeve is small by modern standards — a 15-inch laptop won't fit, limiting the bag's utility as a work/travel hybrid for larger laptop owners. Consider the 30L version for laptop flexibility.
    • No top-loading access — all camera gear loads through the front flap, which requires laying the bag down to fully access the main compartment. Side zip and MagLatch access cover most use cases, but traditional top-loaders may prefer alternatives.
    • The side access opening requires knowing bag orientation — finding the side zip while the bag is on your back has a small learning curve compared to shoulder-sling bags.
