    ✅ What Works

    • FOREO's T-Sonic pulsation technology delivers 8,000 micro-pulsations per minute through medical-grade silicone bristles. Unlike bristle brush cleansers (Clarisonic's approach), silicone doesn't harbor bacteria between uses, doesn't shed bristles, and doesn't scratch the skin — it physically dislodges dirt and makeup residue through vibration rather than mechanical abrasion.
    • The dual-sided design serves two distinct functions: the front face cleanses using sonic pulsations, while the back applies the same technology in a slower firming mode designed to stimulate facial muscle tone. Most users use the firming function 2–3 minutes after cleansing, and many report visible improvement in skin firmness over 60–90 days of consistent use.
    • 650 uses per charge (USB-C) means roughly 13 months of daily use before you even need to charge. This drastically outperforms brush-based cleansers that require charging weekly. The battery indicator in the app shows remaining charge so you're never surprised.
    • Medical-grade silicone is inherently non-porous — it cannot harbor bacteria the way foam or bristle heads can. This makes the LUNA 4 genuinely more hygienic than rotary brush cleansers, particularly for users prone to breakouts from bacterial contamination of their cleaning tool.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Results from the firming mode require consistent daily use for 60–90 days before meaningful visible improvement. FOREO is transparent about this timeline, but buyers expecting overnight firmness results will be disappointed. Treat it as a long-term skincare tool, not a quick fix.
    • The LUNA 4 comes in four variants for different skin types (normal, sensitive, combination, oily). Choosing the wrong variant isn't catastrophic — the pulsation intensity adjustable via the app — but choosing the variant matched to your skin type optimizes results from the start.
    • At $279 CAD, this is a significant investment in a cleansing device. The one-year+ battery life and silicone longevity (FOREO devices last 5–10 years with normal use) make the per-year cost reasonable, but the upfront price is higher than most cleanser brushes.
    • The FOREO For You app provides personalized cleansing routines based on your skin type and concerns, but the device works perfectly well without the app using its physical button. App integration is useful but not required, unlike some smart devices that are crippled without connectivity.
