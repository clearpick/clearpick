    ✅ What Works

    • The Chatelet Air 2.0's polycarbonate shell is notably more impact-resistant and flex-tolerant than ABS plastic alternatives—it dents and cracks significantly harder, which matters for frequent flyers whose bags are checked overhead or gate-checked
    • DELSEY's signature double spinner wheels are wide-set and smooth-rolling on both airport linoleum and rough pavement; the silky roll quality is consistently cited as a step above single-wheel spinner alternatives
    • The interior organization—with dual-panel butterfly opening, zippered mesh pockets, and compression straps—is more thoughtfully laid out than budget hardshell competitors that offer only two flat compartments
    • DELSEY's vintage-inspired design and French brand heritage make it genuinely stylish in a category where most carry-ons look identical; the herringbone pattern interior and matte exterior finish age better than shiny surfaces

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • At 6.4 lbs empty it is on the heavier side for a carry-on; Travelpro Maxlite 5 softside is 1.4 lbs lighter, which matters on weight-strict airlines that impose a 10 kg combined weight limit
    • The expansion adds only 2 cm (versus 2 inches on many U.S. brands)—useful for a thin extra layer but not a meaningful volume increase for packing heavy
    • DELSEY's 10-year international warranty is strong on paper but the claims process through Canadian retail channels can be slow; front-end quality is high enough that most buyers never need it

    Common complaints

    Heavier than comparably priced softside options

    At 6.4 lbs the polycarbonate shell contributes significant dead weight on strict 10 kg carry-on limits. Experienced frequent flyers who track total weight closely often prefer Travelpro or away softside alternatives.

    Source: Reddit r/travel

    Interior space feels smaller than dimensions suggest

    The butterfly-opening interior layout with center-mounted pockets divides the main cavity into two narrower sections. Wide items—folded dress shirts, large toiletry bags—feel cramped compared to a single open-panel case.

    Source: Amazon.ca reviewer

    Combination lock is stiff initially

    Several reviewers note the built-in combination lock requires significant force to set during the first few uses and the default combination isn't obvious from the manual. Takes a few weeks to break in smoothly.

    Source: Amazon.ca reviewer
    ClearPick Verdict

    The DELSEY Chatelet Air 2.0 is the carry-on for travelers who want premium aesthetics and durability without reaching Away or Rimowa territory. The polycarbonate construction is genuinely more durable than budget ABS shells, the double-wheel spinners are a pleasure on long terminal walks, and the design is distinctive without being garish. Weight-conscious frequent flyers on strict 10 kg limits should check the softside Travelpro alternatives first. For occasional to moderate travelers who want quality that looks good, this delivers.
