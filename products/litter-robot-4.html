    ✅ What Works

    • The fully automated cleaning cycle fires 30 minutes after each use, rotating the globe to sift waste into the sealed drawer below. Most owners report near-zero manual scooping — the unit handles everything, and the sealed waste drawer contains odor far better than an open box with a lid.
    • The OdorTrap system with activated carbon filter is the most effective smell management in any automatic litter box. Multi-cat households that previously battled litter odor report a dramatic improvement, with guests often unable to detect a litter box is present at all.
    • The WhiskerApp provides granular usage data — visit counts, weight trends, and cycle history for each cat. Over time this becomes a genuine health monitoring tool: deviations from normal patterns have alerted owners to urinary issues and kidney disease before symptoms appeared.
    • Build quality is in a different league from budget self-cleaning boxes. The globe mechanism is robust and quiet, the drawer seals tightly, and Litter-Robot's customer support and warranty are consistently praised. Units bought in 2016 still run for owners in 2026.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • The $699 CAD entry price is steep — nearly 10× a basic litter box. The cost calculation shifts if you have multiple cats, hate scooping, or travel frequently, but buyers expecting to feel good about the price immediately are often surprised by sticker shock at checkout.
    • Some cats refuse to use the globe-shaped enclosure entirely, particularly older cats set in their ways or cats who dislike the enclosed space. Litter-Robot offers a trial period, but if your cat rejects the form factor there's no easy fix.
    • Clumping litter is non-negotiable — crystal, non-clumping clay, and pine pellets won't work with the sifting mechanism. If your cat is already on a non-clumping litter, switching litter types alongside introducing a new box can cause further transition resistance.
    • The waste drawer needs emptying every 7–10 days for a single cat, more often with multiple cats. The replacement carbon filters and waste drawer liners are a recurring cost — budget roughly $5–8/month CAD for consumables.
