    ✅ What Works

    • 450°F Max Crisp mode runs hotter than most competitors — fries and wings come out crunchier without added oil, and owners consistently report better results than mid-range models with a lower temperature ceiling.
    • Ceramic nonstick basket is dishwasher-safe and holds up over repeated use — food residue releases easily with a wipe, and owners report the coating remains intact after a year of daily use.
    • Seven-button digital panel requires no learning curve — preheats in 2–3 minutes and the controls are intuitive enough for first-time air fryer buyers.
    • 105°F low end enables proper dehydrating — one of the few models that can run cool enough for dehydrating fruit and jerky without burning edges.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Fan is noticeably louder than the Cosori TurboBlaze — audible from the next room when spinning at high speed. Most owners say it isn't disruptive but it's a consistent comparison point.
    • Large footprint at 14 inches deep — buyers who underestimate counter space regret it. Measure before ordering.
    • No viewing window — you pull the basket to check doneness, which interrupts cooking and is the most consistently mentioned design limitation.
    • Broil rack is sold separately — the base accessory kit is minimal compared to some competitors at this price point.
