    ✅ What Works

    • DC motor with 5-speed fan cooks up to 46% faster than previous Cosori AC-motor models — wings and tater tots crisp without shaking the basket mid-cook.
    • Quieter operation than the Ninja AF161 — the snail-shell reflector design measurably reduces fan noise, and owners who switched specifically cite this as the reason.
    • PFAS-free ceramic basket is easy to clean — food residue wipes away with soap and water with no scrubbing, and the basket is dishwasher-safe.
    • Proof mode at 90°F and a 24-hour timer range cover use cases most air fryers can't — you can proof bread dough at low temperature before baking.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • No viewing window — must pull basket to check doneness, which interrupts cooking. The most consistently mentioned design complaint from owners.
    • Uneven results on some foods until you learn the 5-speed fan — chips and thin items cooked inconsistently in early tests before dialing in the right settings.
    • Large center hole in the crisping tray allows small foods (Brussels sprout halves, small potato pieces) to fall through.
    • Cosori had a 2023 voluntary recall on earlier models for a fire hazard. The TurboBlaze is a new design and is not affected, but the brand history is worth knowing.
