    ✅ What Works

    • The DEWALT Accessory Interface (DAI) is the most important feature of this platform. It accepts DEWALT's own 80+ blades and attachments, but also accepts all OIS-standard accessories from Fein, Bosch, Milwaukee, and others — meaning you're not locked into one brand's accessory ecosystem and can use whatever blade type you find locally.
    • Variable speed from 8,000 to 20,000 OPM covers the full range of oscillating tool tasks. Low speed (8-12,000 OPM) handles delicate sanding, scraping paint from trim, and flush-cutting near finished surfaces. High speed (16-20,000 OPM) drives through drywall cuts, plunge cuts in softwood, and grout removal.
    • The 3-zone grip design — three distinct hand positions along the body — is a genuine ergonomic advantage over tools with a single grip zone. Repositioning your hand to a more natural angle for overhead, side-cut, and standard floor-level cutting reduces wrist fatigue during extended tasks like tile grout removal.
    • The 2.0Ah battery included with the D1 kit (as opposed to tool-only models) provides meaningful runtime — roughly 30-45 minutes of continuous oscillating work — with the full DEWALT 20V MAX ecosystem compatibility. The kit is the right buy for users without an existing battery collection.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Oscillating multi-tools excel at precision tasks — flush cutting, grout removal, undercutting door casings, trim sanding — but they're not fast material removers. For tasks requiring significant wood removal (dadoes, mortises, wide cuts), a router or jigsaw is faster. The multi-tool's value is in the tasks no other tool handles cleanly.
    • The 3.0° oscillating angle is narrower than some competing tools (like Fein's 3.1°). The narrower angle produces finer, more precise cuts but can feel slower in aggressive material removal compared to higher-angle competitors. It's a design choice favouring precision over speed.
    • Blade life varies enormously by material and task. Grout removal consumes carbide blades quickly — expect 30-60 minutes of grout life per blade depending on hardness. Wood-cutting blades last far longer. Having spare blades on hand is essential for tile and grout projects.
    • Like all oscillating tools, the DCS356D1 generates vibration — more than drills or sanders. Extended use (over an hour continuously) will fatigue hands and wrists. Professional users doing full-day grout removal or trim undercutting work benefit from anti-vibration gloves.
