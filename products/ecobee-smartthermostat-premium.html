    ✅ What Works

    • SmartSensor's room-level occupancy detection is the strongest differentiator — the thermostat only conditions rooms with people in them, and owners consistently cite this as meaningfully reducing their energy bills compared to a traditional programmable thermostat.
    • Broadest multi-platform compatibility of any smart thermostat — works with Alexa, Google Assistant, Siri/HomeKit, SmartThings, and IFTTT simultaneously. Unlike Nest, it doesn't lock out any major platform.
    • No mandatory subscription — all core features including scheduling, SmartSensor, geofencing, energy reports, and all voice assistant integrations work without any monthly fee.
    • Clean installation with included C-wire adapter and step-by-step app wiring guide. The base plate has a built-in level. Most owners report completing installation without an electrician.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Eco+ feature can override your temperature setpoint based on utility grid demand and off-peak pricing. It's opt-in and free, but many owners are surprised to find it enabled by default and come home to an unexpected temperature. Review settings carefully after setup.
    • Built-in speaker is weak — roughly equivalent to a first-generation Echo Dot. It works for voice commands but is not suitable for music or as an Alexa speaker replacement.
    • Air quality monitor shows VOC and CO2 on a coarse Clean-to-High scale with no fine particulate (PM2.5) data, and cannot yet trigger smart home automations like air purifiers through Alexa or HomeKit.
    • Additional SmartSensors are sold only in 2-packs at $99+ — you can't buy a single sensor if you only need one more room covered.
