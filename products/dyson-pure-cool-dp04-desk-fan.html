    ✅ What Works

    • HEPA filtration in a desk-sized form factor is a unique combination — no other compact desk fan provides true HEPA air purification. For offices, bedrooms, and nurseries where a full-size floor unit is too large, the DP04's footprint is a genuine advantage.
    • Bladeless Air Multiplier design is safe for desks with children or pets — no exposed blades that can cause injury from accidental contact, and easy to clean without disassembling a blade assembly.
    • App and remote control allows changing fan speed and oscillation without touching the unit — useful when the DP04 is mounted on a nightstand and you want to adjust settings while lying in bed.
    • 350° programmable oscillation covers most room directions from a desk position — the programmable arc lets you focus airflow where you need it rather than oscillating past empty walls.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • $479 for a desk fan is a premium price — the Dyson Pure Cool DP04 is significantly more expensive than combining a small Levoit air purifier and a standard desk fan, which would cost under $150 for comparable coverage.
    • Filter replacement at ~$70/yr is the same ongoing cost as the HP09 and TP09 — Dyson's filter pricing doesn't scale down for smaller units.
    • Airflow strength is limited compared to floor tower models — the compact size produces less raw airflow volume than the Dyson TP09 or dedicated fans. For hot summer cooling, a standalone tower fan moves more air.
    • Oscillation range is smaller than floor models — 350° is the maximum rotation, covering most but not all room directions when placed on a desk away from walls.

    ClearPick Verdict

    The DP04 is the right product for a specific scenario: a desk or nightstand where you want both air purification and a fan, and you can't or don't want a full-size floor unit.
