    ✅ What Works

    • The 56V brushless motor handles thick grass and weeds at the base of fences and along garden bed edges without the stalling that affects lower-voltage trimmers. Owners regularly cut through a week of overgrowth without reducing speed.
    • Carbon fibre composite shaft is lightweight but rigid — no flex or wobble during heavy cutting. The split-shaft design also accepts EGO attachment accessories, converting the trimmer into an edger, cultivator, or pole saw with separate heads.
    • EGO 56V battery compatibility means this trimmer uses the same batteries as the LM2156SP mower and LB6504 blower. For EGO ecosystem users, it rounds out a complete battery-powered yard tool set on a single charger.
    • Weather-sealed construction handles Canadian spring and fall conditions — light rain and wet grass are not an issue, which matters for a tool that's often used right after mowing or in damp morning conditions.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Battery and charger inclusion varies by listing — the ST1521S is available in kit (with 2.5Ah battery + charger) and tool-only versions. Confirm before ordering; 56V EGO batteries run $70–$130 CAD separately.
    • At ~7 lb with battery, this is heavier than entry-level trimmers. Extended sessions (45+ minutes) along large property edges can cause arm and shoulder fatigue.
    • .095" line is the standard diameter for residential trimming but not the heaviest available — for thick brush or heavy overgrowth, a larger diameter line (available as replacement spools) would improve performance.
    • Bump feed requires correctly loaded line to function reliably. Owners who load line incorrectly report feed jams — the first few loads have a learning curve.
