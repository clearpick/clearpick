    ✅ What Works

    • At 5.0 lbs, the Maxlite 5 is among the lightest carry-on spinners available—1.9 lbs lighter than the Samsonite Omni 2, which translates directly to 1.9 lbs more clothing or gear allowed within a 10 kg carry-on limit (WestJet, Porter) or more headroom before exceeding weight at check-in
    • Travelpro's spinner wheels are the airline-rated standard used by flight crews—the pilot trolley brand's quality carries into the consumer line, and the wheels maintain smooth operation over rough airport tiles and outdoor pavement where cheaper wheels seize
    • The hang-up hook on the interior packing panel lets you hang the bag in a hotel closet with it open for visibility and access, an organizational feature absent from most competitor carry-ons that requires separate packing cubes to achieve
    • High-density nylon softside construction allows the bag to flex into overhead bins when partially full, avoiding the size-limit rejection risk that exact-dimension hardshells face when bins are diagonally packed

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Softside nylon shows scuffs and wear patterns after heavy travel use more visibly than hardshell; the dark colorways (black, slate green) hide wear better than lighter options
    • The 2-inch expansion takes the bag to maximum airline tolerance—expanding to full depth may make the bag too large for some regional airline overhead bins; use expansion capacity for checked bag scenarios rather than as routine carry-on packing
    • Travelpro's warranty claims are handled through the U.S. for Canadian customers, requiring shipping to a service center rather than local Canadian retail resolution

    Common complaints

    Warranty service requires U.S. shipping from Canada

    Travelpro's warranty service center is in the United States. Canadian warranty claims require cross-border shipping, which adds $30–$50 in shipping costs and 2–3 weeks for turnaround. Samsonite Canada warranty resolution is faster for Canadian buyers.

    Source: Reddit r/travel

    Nylon shows wear after 2–3 years of heavy use

    The high-density nylon holds up well but darkens and shows scuff texture after 50+ flights. A hardshell at the same price point maintains a cleaner appearance longer, though this is aesthetic not functional.

    Source: Amazon.ca reviewer

    Front pocket is small

    The exterior front zip pocket is shallow and narrow—fits a document or a small cable wallet but not a standard-size toiletry bag or a bulkier item. Organization outside the main compartment is limited to this one small pocket.

    Source: Amazon.ca reviewer
