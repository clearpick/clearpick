    ✅ What Works

    • The Chicco KeyFit series has consistently topped independent infant seat crash test performance ratings from NHTSA and Canadian testing bodies—it's the benchmark other infant seats are measured against for ease of correct installation and rear-facing safety
    • ReclineSure leveling foot (instead of angle indicator bubbles used by competitors) makes the correct recline angle far easier to achieve; the foot extends until contact and a level indicator turns green—much clearer than aligning bubbles in dim garages
    • The ClearTex fabric is made without harmful chemicals typically found in flame retardants, appealing to parents who want reduced chemical exposure for infants spending significant time in the seat
    • Spring-loaded pop-up canopy extends in three separate panels to provide coverage from multiple sun angles; most competing seats have a single rigid canopy that only blocks one direction effectively

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Infant-only seat with a shorter useful window than convertible seats—most babies outgrow the 35 lb weight or 32-inch height limit between 12–18 months, requiring purchase of a convertible seat sooner than families expect
    • The base is substantial and takes up meaningful floor space in the vehicle; smaller vehicles may struggle with rear-facing installation given the depth of the ReclineSure foot when extended
    • ClearTex fabric is less plush than standard Chicco fabric and some parents find it less soft-looking in photos, though baby comfort is equivalent
