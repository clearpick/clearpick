  • Native Bluetooth connectivity syncs with Peloton app, Zwift, JRNY, and any ANT+ or Bluetooth fitness platform — gives you the Peloton experience at a fraction of the cost by using your own tablet with a $13/month Peloton app subscription instead of $44/month
