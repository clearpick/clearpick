    ✅ What Works

    • 2400 PSI handles most residential cleaning tasks that the Sun Joe SPX3000 leaves unfinished — lightly soiled concrete driveways, weathered deck boards, algae-covered siding, and caked-on mud from a Canadian winter.
    • DEWALT build quality is noticeably more durable than Sun Joe or Greenworks — the housing, hose fittings, and connection points feel like they'll survive multiple seasons of Canadian outdoor storage without degrading.
    • Onboard storage for nozzles, spray wand, and hose keeps everything organized in one unit — a real workflow convenience that avoids the tangled-cord-and-scattered-nozzles problem of budget models.
    • Total Stop System shuts the motor when the trigger is released — preserves pump life meaningfully over the long run compared to continuously-running motors.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • At $329 CAD, it costs $130 more than the Sun Joe SPX3000 — for buyers doing infrequent seasonal cleaning, the price difference may not be justified by the durability improvement.
    • 1.1 GPM flow rate is identical to the Greenworks 1800 PSI and lower than Sun Joe's 1.76 GPM — despite higher PSI, it covers surface area more slowly than the SPX3000 per minute.
    • No foam cannon included — Sun Joe's dual detergent tanks are a better car-washing setup out of the box. A foam cannon attachment for DEWALT is sold separately.
    • Corded electric only — no battery option. Requires access to a 120V outlet within 35 ft of the cleaning area.

    ClearPick Verdict

    The DEWALT DWPW2400 is the right pressure washer for buyers who want durability over specs — 2400 PSI in a housing that feels built to last multiple Canadian seasons, with organized onboard storage that budget models skip. The lower GPM and higher price relative to Sun Joe's SPX3000 are the honest trade: you get a sturdier tool that handles tougher cleaning tasks, but pay more and cover area more slowly. For frequent users or buyers who've burned through budget pressure washers before, the DEWALT is worth the premium.
