    ✅ What Works

    • The MagSpeed electromagnetic scroll wheel is the single best mouse feature available at any price. Silent, near-frictionless, and capable of scrolling 1,000 lines per second in freewheel mode — navigating a 10,000-row spreadsheet, a long codebase, or a dense design file becomes qualitatively different compared to a standard notched wheel.
    • The 8000 DPI optical sensor tracks accurately on glass surfaces — most mouse sensors require a mousepad, but the MX Master 3S works reliably on glass desks, polished wood, and other reflective surfaces that defeat standard optical sensors.
    • SmartShift automatically switches between click-to-click ratchet scrolling and high-speed freewheel based on how fast you scroll. Slow scroll for precise document navigation stays in ratchet mode; fast scroll for long-document traversal drops into freewheel. The transition is instant and requires no manual switching.
    • The ergonomic right-hand grip is shaped around natural hand position for right-handed users with medium-to-large hands. The thumb rest, angled wheel position, and elevated profile distribute grip pressure across the palm rather than concentrating it on the fingers.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • The MX Master 3S is right-hand only — no left-handed version exists. Left-handed users should look at the Logitech Lift Vertical (left-handed version available) or third-party options.
    • Full button customization requires the Logitech Options+ software running in the background. The default button assignments are sensible but the app-specific configurations — where the thumb buttons behave differently in Photoshop versus Chrome versus VS Code — require Logi Options+ to configure.
    • At $139 CAD, this is a premium mouse purchase. The MagSpeed wheel and multi-device switching justify the price for power users; casual users who scroll occasionally and don't need multi-computer support will find less return on the premium.
    • The 70-day battery life with USB-C charging makes this essentially a maintenance-free mouse for most users. The minute-to-3-hours fast charge means even running it to zero is not an inconvenience.
