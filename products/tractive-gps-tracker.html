    ✅ What Works

    • Live Tracking mode updates your dog's location every 2–3 seconds via cellular, making it the closest thing to real-time GPS available in a consumer pet tracker. When a dog bolts or squeezes through a fence gap, you get a near-live position that's genuinely usable for pursuit — not a 30-second-old ping that's already stale.
    • Worldwide cellular coverage means the tracker works anywhere your carrier has a signal, including international travel with your dog. For Canadians who travel to the US or Europe with pets, a single Tractive subscription covers all destinations without roaming complications.
    • The activity monitoring tracks daily steps, calories burned, and sleep quality against breed benchmarks. For owners managing a dog's weight or recovering from surgery, having objective daily activity data rather than a subjective sense of 'did we walk enough today' is genuinely valuable.
    • At 35g the tracker is light enough for medium and large dogs without causing discomfort. The IP67 waterproof rating means it survives rain, mud, and swimming — Tractive owners report durability through multiple Canadian winters without waterproofing failures.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • A subscription is mandatory for any features beyond the device itself — Live Tracking, location history, and geofence alerts all require a paid plan. At $6.99–$12.99/month CAD depending on the plan, the annual cost runs $84–$156 CAD on top of the device price. This is the real cost of ownership.
    • Battery life drops dramatically in Live Tracking mode — from 5–7 days in standard mode to 2–3 days when actively tracking in real-time. If your dog regularly escapes and you run Live Tracking frequently, you'll be charging every other day.
    • GPS accuracy in dense urban environments or heavy tree cover can drift 10–30 metres from actual position. This is inherent to GPS technology, not a Tractive-specific flaw, but owners expecting pinpoint precision in cities or forests may find the circle of uncertainty frustrating.
    • The subscription auto-renews and Tractive's cancellation process has generated complaints about difficulty stopping recurring charges. Setting a calendar reminder to review and a card alert before renewal is advisable.
