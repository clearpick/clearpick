    ✅ What Works

    • The Thermo-Spot heat indicator — a ring that turns solid red when the pan reaches the correct searing temperature — eliminates guesswork about preheat and prevents both under-heated and scorched pans, a feature that genuinely changes how you cook.
    • Titanium-reinforced non-stick coating releases eggs, fish, and delicate proteins cleanly without added fat, and holds up well with proper care (hand wash, silicone or wood utensils, low-to-medium heat).
    • 17 pieces covers every pot and pan need for a fully equipped kitchen — saucepans, frying pans, saute pan, Dutch oven, stockpot, steamer insert, and a full lid set.
    • Hard-anodized construction distributes heat evenly and eliminates hot spots that cause uneven cooking, a common problem in cheaper aluminum sets.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Non-stick coating will wear after 3–5 years of daily use even with proper care — it's not a permanent surface like cast iron, and eventually needs replacement.
    • The set is not induction-compatible — the hard-anodized base does not respond to induction cooktops, which rules it out for a significant portion of modern Canadian kitchens.
    • Oven-safe only to 400°F — lower than cast iron or stainless sets, which limits high-heat finishing techniques like broiling or very high oven roasting.
    • Handle rivets on some units have loosened over time with repeated dishwasher use — the manufacturer recommends hand washing, which is necessary for long-term handle integrity.
