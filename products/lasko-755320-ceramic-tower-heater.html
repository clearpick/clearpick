    • Reliability track record is the defining feature — the Lasko 755320 has been among the top-selling and highest-rated tower heaters on Amazon.ca for years, with thousands of reviews from owners who return to update after 2, 3, and 5+ years. Consistent long-term performance is well documented.
    • Cool-touch exterior is safe for living rooms with children and pets — even at full 1500W output, the outer housing stays cool to the touch, eliminating the burn risk from exposed ceramic surfaces or metal grilles on traditional space heaters.
    • Oscillating tower distributes heat across a room — the rotating tower design spreads warmth in a wide arc, more effective than fixed heaters at creating even temperature distribution in living rooms and open spaces.
    • 8-hour timer with auto-shutoff provides fire safety for overnight and unattended use — set it for 2, 4, or 8 hours and it shuts off automatically, reducing risk from heaters left on accidentally.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • 1500W is the maximum power draw for a 120V outlet heater — all standard electric space heaters share this ceiling. The Lasko's oscillation advantage is in heat distribution, not wattage.
    • Plastic tower exterior is functional but not premium — the build quality matches the price point. Serviceable and durable, but doesn't have the build quality of De'Longhi or Dyson.
    • Fan noise on high heat is audible — the ceramic element requires a fan to distribute heat, and the fan noise is noticeable in quiet environments. Not suitable for silent bedroom operation (see De'Longhi HMP1500 for that use case).
    • No precise digital thermostat — the Lasko uses a dial thermostat rather than digital 1° increment control. Acceptable for most users, but less precise than premium models.
