Breville Barista Express (BES870XL)

What Buyers Say

✅ What Works

  • Built-in conical burr grinder grinds directly into the portafilter — eliminates the need for a separate grinder and the counter space, cost, and workflow complexity that comes with it
  • Dose Control Grinding delivers the right amount of coffee for a single or double shot automatically — removes one of the most common beginner mistakes in home espresso
  • PID temperature control maintains precise water temperature throughout extraction — the consistency this delivers is what separates it from cheaper machines that produce variable shot quality
  • Build quality rivals machines costing $300-500 more — the metal construction, solid portafilter, and quality components throughout suggest a machine built to last with proper care

⚠️ Worth Knowing

  • The built-in grinder is the convenience trade-off — espresso enthusiasts consistently note that a separate quality grinder paired with the Bambino Plus produces better shots, though the Express is significantly more convenient
  • Learning curve is real — pulling consistently good espresso takes practice and dialling in the grind takes time, particularly for first-time espresso machine owners
  • Hot water output for Americanos comes out in short bursts rather than a continuous stream — a minor but documented quirk that regular Americano drinkers notice
