    ✅ What Works

    • 50h battery (ANC off) is among the best in the over-ear category — for frequent travelers and commuters who forget to charge, the Tour One M2 delivers a level of battery anxiety-free use that Sony and Bose can't match.
    • JBL Personi-Fi 2.0 runs a personalized hearing test and calibrates EQ to your specific hearing profile — the improvement in perceived clarity is noticeable for most users, particularly at the frequency range where individual hearing varies most.
    • Multipoint Bluetooth allows simultaneous connection to a laptop and phone — calls switch instantly between devices without manual re-pairing, which is genuinely useful for work-from-home setups.
    • Adaptive ANC with three modes (everyday, travel, and off) handles both office noise and airplane engine hum well. Performance sits between the Bose QC45 and Sony XM6 in real-world commuting tests.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • The Personi-Fi calibration test takes 5 minutes and is worthwhile but opt-in — buyers who skip it get a slightly V-shaped sound signature (boosted bass and highs) that not everyone prefers.
    • At 289g, these are heavier than the Sony WH-1000XM6 (254g) and Bose QC45 (238g). Extended sessions of 4+ hours cause noticeable fatigue for some listeners.
    • The JBL app is functional but has been inconsistently reviewed for stability — force quits and occasional connection drops to the app have been reported across iOS and Android.
    • ANC, while good, doesn't quite reach the Sony WH-1000XM6 level of environmental noise reduction in loud transit environments. The gap is small but real at the price point.

    ClearPick Verdict

    The JBL Tour One M2 is the strongest JBL has ever been in the premium over-ear category, with 50h battery, Personi-Fi sound personalization, and competitive adaptive ANC. Battery life is the clear standout — nothing in this price range lasts as long. The weight and app stability issues are real drawbacks for long listening sessions and power users. For buyers who prioritize battery endurance and personalized sound over the last word in ANC, this is the best JBL headphone available.
