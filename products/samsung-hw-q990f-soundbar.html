    ✅ What Works

    • True 11.1.4 surround with wireless rears included in the box — this is a full home theater audio system that competes with dedicated AV receivers at a fraction of the complexity. The wireless rear speakers with upfiring drivers create genuinely immersive overhead surround
    • HDMI 2.1 passthrough means you can run your devices through the soundbar without losing video quality or HDR — a feature the Sonos Arc Ultra lacks entirely at a similar price
    • DTS:X and Dolby Atmos both supported — physical Blu-ray collections with DTS-HD tracks work at full quality, something Sonos can't offer
    • Q-Symphony integration with Samsung TVs turns your TV speakers into additional channels — if you own a compatible Samsung TV, the combined system is genuinely more immersive than the soundbar alone

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • The 2024 Q990D was bricked by a firmware update — Samsung issued free repairs, but the incident raised reliability concerns about auto-updates on premium hardware. The Q990F addresses this with improved update protocols, but it's in the product's recent history
    • Setup complexity is real — 11 channels with wireless rears, a subwoofer, and SmartThings integration is more involved than a single-bar system. The payoff is worth it for home theater enthusiasts, but casual users may find it overwhelming
    • Rear speaker placement matters significantly — the wireless rears need to be within 4-5 feet of the listening position and have a clear line of sight to the main bar to maintain wireless connection. Not all room layouts accommodate this cleanly
    • Physical footprint is substantial — the subwoofer cube (redesigned for Q990F to be more compact) still requires floor space near your TV setup

    Common complaints

    Firmware OTA bricking incident (Q990D) left hundreds of units inoperable

    The Q990D firmware bricking incident (an OTA update rendered hundreds of units inoperable, requiring factory service) is the most significant complaint in r/soundbars — Samsung offered free repairs but the trust damage lingers. The Q990F is the follow-up model with improved update safeguards.

    Source: r/soundbars, Digital Trends, TechRadar reporting on Q990D firmware incident

    Wireless rear speaker connectivity drops under interference conditions

    Wireless rear speaker connectivity drops under certain interference conditions — multiple owners in r/hometheater report occasional stuttering or dropout in the rear channels, particularly in apartments with dense Wi-Fi environments.

    Source: r/hometheater, r/soundbars

    Dialogue clarity inconsistent at low night-listening volumes

    Dialogue clarity at low volumes is inconsistent — a recurring complaint from owners who watch TV late at night at low levels, where the center channel can sound slightly muddy compared to dedicated center-channel speaker setups.

    Source: r/hometheater, WhatHiFi Q990F review

    The Samsung HW-Q990F is the most complete soundbar system available in Canada — 11.1.4 channels, wireless rears, HDMI passthrough, and full DTS:X + Dolby Atmos support in one box at $1,298 CAD.
