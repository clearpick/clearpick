    ✅ What Works

    • LDAC Hi-Res Audio support at $79 CAD is extraordinary value — a codec typically reserved for headphones at 3-4× the price delivers noticeably better wireless audio quality on compatible Android devices.
    • 50h battery (ANC off) rivals headphones costing $300+ more. For budget-conscious buyers who hate charging anxiety, the Q45 is essentially set-and-forget for a full workweek.
    • Three adaptive ANC modes tune noise cancellation for your environment — Transport mode for transit and planes, Indoor for office use, and Outdoor for open spaces. The adaptation is genuine and effective for the price tier.
    • Multipoint Bluetooth allows simultaneous connection to two devices — laptop and phone work together without re-pairing, a feature that often costs $200+ on competing headphones.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • ANC performance doesn't approach Sony WH-1000XM6 or Bose QuietComfort 45 — at $79 vs $350+, this is expected, but buyers comparing ANC quality will notice the gap in loud transit environments.
    • LDAC requires a compatible Android device and a LDAC-capable source — iPhone users and many Android users without LDAC support won't benefit from the Hi-Res certification.
    • The earcup memory foam compresses noticeably after 2-3 hours of continuous wear. Long listening sessions can cause discomfort around the ears that premium headphones with deeper cups avoid.
    • Build quality reflects the price — the plastic chassis is lightweight but doesn't feel premium. Travel stress and drops may cause issues over time that more expensive aluminum-framed competitors resist.

    ClearPick Verdict

    The Anker Soundcore Q45 punches far above its $79 CAD price by offering LDAC Hi-Res Audio, 50h battery, three-mode adaptive ANC, and multipoint connectivity — a feature set that costs $300+ on premium competitors. ANC won't satisfy Sony or Bose expectations, and earcup comfort falls off after long sessions. For budget-conscious buyers, students, or anyone wanting capable everyday headphones without spending $300, the Q45 is the clear value leader in the over-ear category.
