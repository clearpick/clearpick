    ✅ What Works

    • 14-cup capacity covers large households and office settings without multiple brew cycles — owners regularly run a full pot for morning gatherings without any issue.
    • The programmable 24-hour timer means coffee is ready when you wake up, and the bold brew setting produces noticeably stronger coffee that holds up to milk without tasting watered down.
    • The 1–4 cup small batch mode adjusts the brew to avoid over-extraction when you only want one or two cups — a genuinely useful feature most drip machines skip.
    • At under $90 CAD, it delivers reliable, consistent results that rival machines in the $150+ range, making it the clearest value pick in the drip coffee category.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • The glass carafe develops staining over time and is harder to clean than stainless thermal carafes — a bottle brush is essentially required.
    • The 60-minute auto shutoff is fixed and will shut the machine off before you finish the pot if you brew early and drink slowly.
    • Keep-warm at higher temperature settings can slightly scorch the bottom of the coffee over 30–40 minutes, introducing a mild bitter edge.
    • This is a straightforward drip machine — no grinder, no bloom pause, no temperature control. Coffee quality is ceiling-capped by the ground coffee you use.
