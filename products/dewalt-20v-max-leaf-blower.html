    ✅ What Works

    • At 2.9 lb without battery, this is one of the lightest leaf blowers you can buy — a meaningful advantage for extended cleanup sessions, overhead blowing, or users with arm fatigue concerns.
    • The DEWALT 20V MAX battery system is the most widely used cordless power tool platform in North America. Buyers with DEWALT drills, circular saws, or impact drivers can add this blower to their existing battery ecosystem at tool-only cost.
    • 100 CFM and 90 MPH is adequate for regular patio, deck, driveway, and garage cleanup — the jobs most homeowners actually do most often. It handles dry leaves on flat surfaces well.
    • Three-speed switch plus variable trigger makes precision easy — low speed for fine dust and light debris without scattering everything, high for moving larger accumulations.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • 100 CFM is modest by battery blower standards — EGO runs at 650 CFM, Greenworks 40V at 150 CFM. For large property leaf clearing or heavy wet piles, this blower will require more time and passes.
    • The (B) suffix indicates tool-only — battery sold separately. Most buyers in the DEWALT 20V ecosystem already own compatible batteries, but new buyers need to budget for a battery and charger.
    • This is not the tool for large-property leaf blowing, clearing gutters, or moving heavy wet debris. It excels at light, frequent cleanup rather than major autumn leaf events.
    • 90 MPH is air speed, not the same metric as CFM (volume). High MPH with low CFM — like this blower — means a focused fast stream that moves light debris but lacks the volume to push heavy piles efficiently.
