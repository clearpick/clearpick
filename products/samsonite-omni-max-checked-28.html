    ✅ What Works

    • The 28-inch checked size provides the volume most families need for a 7–14 day international trip for one person, or a week for two people sharing a large checked bag, without requiring multiple checked bags at $35+ per flight
    • Samsonite's Omni 2 shell resilience (polycarbonate/ABS blend) handles the abuse of airport baggage handling better than softside alternatives—drag damage, conveyor system impacts, and rough sorting at busy hubs leave cosmetic scuffs but the structural integrity holds
    • The matching Omni 2 carry-on creates a complete matching set with identical spinner quality, TSA lock design, and shell color for travelers who want coordinated luggage aesthetics
    • The 1.5-inch expansion adds practical volume for souvenir-heavy return trips, and the interior cross-strap divider keeps the expanded contents from shifting in transit

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • At 9.5 lbs empty, the bag itself uses 9.5 of your 23 kg checked allowance (Air Canada/WestJet standard) before adding any clothing—heavy packers who fill a 28-inch bag may approach the weight limit with moderate contents
    • 28-inch bags are not accepted as carry-on by any major airline; this is strictly checked luggage—if you want a bag that can be both, the 20-inch carry-on is the option
    • Hardshell provides better water protection than softside when checked in rain tarmacs, but the zipper expansion area is not waterproof and heavy rain can seep in at the expanded zipper section

    Common complaints

    Heavy before any packing begins

    9.5 lbs empty leaves less than 14 lbs of packing room before hitting a 23 kg limit, fully packed. Travelers who pack densely and have a lot of shoes or heavy clothing run into limit problems more often with this than softside alternatives.

    Source: Reddit r/travel

    Shell scratches visibly over time

    The ABS component of the hardshell blend picks up surface scratches after 10–15 trips through baggage handling. These are cosmetic and don't affect function, but the bag ages visually faster than travelers expect.

    Source: Amazon.ca reviewer

    Expansion zipper less sealed against water

    When expanded, the zipper area around the expansion is less weather-resistant. Checked in heavy rain tarmac conditions, moisture can occasionally seep into contents packed near the expansion perimeter.

    Source: Amazon.ca reviewer
    ClearPick Verdict

    The Samsonite Omni 2 28-inch checked bag is the right choice for families and frequent travelers who want Samsonite's proven spinner quality and shell resilience in a full checked-bag size without reaching premium brand pricing. Heavy packers should weigh the empty 9.5 lbs carefully against their airline's checked allowance.
