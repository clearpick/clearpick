    ✅ What Works

    • At 1700 peak amps with 46-inch industrial cables, the JNC660 can start large diesel trucks, commercial vehicles, and farm equipment that lithium lithium jump starters simply don't have the sustained cranking power to handle
    • The sealed lead-acid battery holds a charge for months without active maintenance, making it reliable for seasonal use—leave it in the garage all winter and it will still start a dead battery in spring
    • Clore's professional-grade cable clamps are heavy copper jaws rather than the thin-metal clips found on consumer units; they grip battery terminals securely and handle the heat and current of repeated commercial use
    • Shop and fleet operators trust the JNC660 specifically because its lead-acid design can absorb the abuse of daily multi-vehicle commercial use without the lithium safety management limitations that trigger protection cutoffs

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • At 18 lbs, this is a heavy unit that requires two hands and a clear trunk to manage—lithium jump starters like the NOCO GB40 achieve 1000 amps at 1 lb and fit in a glovebox, which is a real convenience advantage for most passenger car owners
    • The lead-acid battery requires periodic top-up charging (every 30 days if stored) to avoid sulfation damage that permanently reduces capacity; a maintenance charger is a useful companion purchase
    • No USB charging port, no air compressor, no digital display—this is a pure jump starter with no multi-function extras; the price is largely for raw power and cable quality
