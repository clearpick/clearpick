    ✅ What Works

    • Fluid pan-and-tilt head at under $100 is the defining feature — buttery-smooth horizontal and vertical panning makes this tripod suitable for video shooting in a way most cheap tripods are not. Competing tripods in this price range typically include friction ball heads that make smooth video pans impossible.
    • 5kg payload genuinely handles any mirrorless or APS-C DSLR with a standard zoom — the rating is conservative and the tripod is stable under that load without wobbling in normal indoor conditions.
    • 38cm folded length fits in most camera bag side pockets or checked luggage without special cases — meaningfully more compact than full-size travel tripods.
    • Quick-lock flip levers adjust and secure leg sections faster than twist-lock alternatives — a practical advantage when setting up quickly in the field.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Two-section legs don't get as compact as 4- or 5-section travel tripods — the Manfrotto packs to 38cm, while specialized travel tripods like the Joby and others can reach under 30cm for jacket pockets.
    • At maximum 146cm height, there is noticeable wobble in windy outdoor conditions — the lighter aluminum build trades some stability for weight and compactness.
    • Not carbon fiber at this price point — the aluminum construction is heavier than premium carbon fiber travel tripods. Acceptable for most users, but dedicated travelers may prefer carbon.
    • Fluid head panning is smooth but not tension-adjustable — the fluid resistance is fixed, which suits most users but can't be fine-tuned for very slow or very fast pans.
