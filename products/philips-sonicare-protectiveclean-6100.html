    ✅ What Works

    • The ProtectiveClean 6100 delivers the same core 31,000 strokes/minute sonic technology as the $299 DiamondClean 9300 at half the price. The clinical plaque removal performance is equivalent — the premium you pay for the DiamondClean goes to additional modes, the glass charger, longer battery, and app integration, not to the fundamental cleaning ability.
    • Three modes (Clean, White, Gum Care) cover the most important use cases. The Gum Care mode is the most clinically significant — it runs at a reduced intensity specifically for users with sensitive or receding gums, and it's the mode most dentists are referring to when they recommend Sonicare for sensitive gum patients.
    • The BrushSync technology detects the installed brush head via a color-coded sensor and automatically selects the appropriate brushing program. When the brush head is due for replacement (at 3 months), the indicator fades — removing the need to remember when you last changed heads, which most people genuinely can't track.
    • The pressure sensor that pauses the brush when excess pressure is detected is the same safety mechanism as higher-end Sonicare models. This is the most important protective feature for long-term gum and enamel health, and the ProtectiveClean 6100's inclusion of it makes it a complete and responsible toothbrush at a mid-range price.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • There's no app connectivity in the ProtectiveClean 6100. If you want real-time coverage mapping and AI brushing coaching, you need to step up to the DiamondClean 9300. For users who just want to brush well without digital supervision, the absence of app features is irrelevant — but know you're giving it up.
    • Two-week battery life is the key downgrade from the DiamondClean's 3 weeks. For most people charging every 2 weeks is a trivial inconvenience, but if you travel frequently without your charger this is worth noting. A full charge takes 24 hours from empty.
    • Included brush heads are standard Premium brush heads, not the specialized Premium All-in-One heads included with the DiamondClean. Both are effective for daily cleaning; the All-in-One heads have additional whitening and gum stimulation zones that the standard heads lack.
    • At $149 CAD with two brush heads, this is meaningfully more expensive than the Oral-B Pro 1000 at $79. The ProtectiveClean 6100's advantage is specifically Sonicare's sonic gentle cleaning approach for users whose dentist has specifically recommended Sonicare — otherwise the Oral-B Pro 1000 is a stronger value.
