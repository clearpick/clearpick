    ✅ What Works

    • Blood glucose trend monitoring is Samsung's breakthrough feature — non-invasive glucose tracking (trend, not absolute measurement) gives diabetics and health-focused users insight no Apple Watch offers.
    • ECG + Samsung Health Monitor app enables AFib detection — health monitoring comparable to Apple Watch at $200 less for Android users who don't need iPhone integration.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Best experience on Samsung Galaxy phones — full integration of Samsung Health, Galaxy AI, and phone link features requires a Samsung phone. Works with non-Samsung Android but with reduced functionality.
    • Blood glucose tracking is trend-only, not medical-grade — not approved for medical diagnosis; designed for lifestyle awareness, not diabetes management.

    Wear OS has fewer watch apps than Apple Watch

    The Galaxy Watch runs Wear OS; the app ecosystem is smaller than Apple Watch's watchOS. Some niche fitness apps are iOS/Apple Watch only.

    Source: Reddit (r/smartwatch)
    ClearPick Verdict

    The Samsung Galaxy Watch 7 is the best Android smartwatch — especially for Samsung phone users who get full Galaxy ecosystem integration. ECG, blood glucose trends, and 30-hour battery make it a comprehensive health tracker. For Android users who don't own a Samsung phone, the Garmin Venu 3's platform-agnostic compatibility and 14-day battery make it a stronger alternative.
