    ✅ What Works

    • Three devices in one unit saves significant floor space and reduces clutter — replacing a separate air purifier, space heater, and fan with one unit that does all three effectively. For small apartments or bedrooms with limited space, this is a genuine practical benefit.
    • Dyson's HEPA H13 sealed filtration system is genuinely excellent — the 360° sealed design prevents air from bypassing the filter, and the catalytic formaldehyde filter continuously destroys formaldehyde without needing replacement.
    • 1°C thermostat precision for heating is meaningfully better than coarser thermostats on cheaper heaters — set 21°C and the unit maintains 21°C accurately, not within a 2-3°C band.
    • Real-time air quality LCD shows PM 2.5, PM 10, VOCs, NO2, humidity, and temperature — providing actionable data about your indoor environment that influences when and how hard the purification needs to run.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • $849 is a significant premium over separate best-in-class devices — a Coway AP-1512HH (air purifier) + Vornado MVH (heater) + Honeywell tower fan costs roughly $350-400 combined and arguably performs comparably for each function individually.
    • Filter replacement at ~$70/yr is an ongoing cost — Dyson's replacement filters are expensive compared to Coway and Levoit alternatives. Calculate total cost of ownership over 3-5 years before comparing price.
    • MyDyson app stores usage data in the cloud — buyers with data privacy concerns should review Dyson's privacy policy, as air quality and usage data is transmitted and stored on Dyson's servers.
    • Fan cooling performance is not as powerful as a dedicated Dyson Cool — the HP09 prioritizes air purification over raw airflow, so it's less effective as a primary cooling fan in very hot weather.

    ClearPick Verdict

    The Dyson HP09 is an excellent product if space consolidation and Dyson's build quality are priorities. The air purification is among the best available, the heating is precise, and the real-time air quality display is genuinely useful. The $849 price and $70/yr filter cost are real considerations.
