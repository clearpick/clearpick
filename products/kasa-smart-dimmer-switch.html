    ✅ What Works

    • The HS220 replaces a standard dimmer switch with a smart one that keeps the physical touch controls — you can still dim with a tap or swipe on the switch itself, without pulling out your phone. Voice control, app scheduling, and automation layer on top of the physical control without replacing it.
    • Dimming quality on compatible dimmable LED bulbs is smooth from 1% to 100% with no flicker, buzzing, or dropout at low levels — issues that plague cheaper dimmers. At $39 CAD, the dimming performance rivals switches that cost twice as much.
    • App scheduling is genuinely useful for home lighting: sunrise/sunset-triggered dimming, away mode, and vacation randomization all work reliably and are easy to configure. The Kasa app handles basic automations well without requiring a separate hub.
    • The switch's physical design fits a standard single-gang electrical box and uses a decora-style faceplate, matching most modern switch covers. Installation takes about 20 minutes with a basic understanding of residential wiring.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • A neutral wire is required for the HS220 — homes with older wiring (particularly pre-1990s construction) may not have a neutral wire in the switch box. Check before purchasing; no-neutral alternatives exist but this model specifically requires it.
    • The HS220 is single-pole only — it cannot be used in a 3-way switch configuration where two switches control the same light. Kasa makes a 3-way version (KS230), but buyers often don't realize this limitation until they've already purchased.
    • The 150W LED maximum load means this switch works best with a single fixture or a small grouping of LED bulbs. For a chandelier or multi-bulb track lighting with higher wattage, verify the combined load stays under 150W.
    • Matter support via a firmware update means HomeKit users can control this switch without the Kasa cloud, but initial pairing still requires a Kasa account. HomeKit does not support the dimming percentage display in the Home app with all versions of the firmware.
