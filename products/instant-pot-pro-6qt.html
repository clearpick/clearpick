    ✅ What Works

    • Ten cooking functions in one footprint — replaces pressure cooker, slow cooker, rice cooker, steamer, yogurt maker, and sterilizer. Owners regularly use four to five different modes per week without ever needing a separate appliance.
    • Preheats 20% faster than the original Duo and cooks 70% faster than stovetop methods — beef stew done in under an hour, tamales in 45 minutes.
    • Quiet steam release switch vents steam gradually rather than in a sudden loud hiss — a meaningful improvement over older Instant Pot models for apartments and quiet households.
    • Ships with an extra sealing ring — addresses the most common Instant Pot frustration (odor transfer) before buyers have to find and order accessories.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Slow cooker function runs cooler than a dedicated slow cooker — owners consistently report needing to add 1–2 hours to traditional slow cooker recipes. Not a replacement if slow cooking is your primary use case.
    • Sealing ring absorbs strong food odors (garlic, curry, chili) and can transfer them subtly to subsequent cooks. The included spare ring helps, but odor is a real and ongoing limitation.
    • Stainless steel inner pot is not nonstick — rice sticks without oil or a pre-soak, and cleanup after rice cooking requires more scrubbing than nonstick alternatives.
    • Learning curve is steeper than marketing suggests — liquid requirements, depressurize timing, and program selection aren't intuitive for first-time pressure cooker users. YouTube tutorials are essentially required.
