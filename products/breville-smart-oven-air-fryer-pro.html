    ✅ What Works

    • The air fry function produces results genuinely competitive with dedicated air fryers — chicken wings come out crispy, frozen fries are properly crunchy, and vegetables caramelize without the need for excess oil. Owners who expected compromise frequently report it replaced their standalone air fryer entirely.
    • Element IQ distributes power across five independent heating elements rather than cycling one or two — the result is more even browning and faster preheat than conventional toaster ovens, particularly noticeable on baked goods and roasted vegetables.
    • At 0.8 cubic feet, this fits a 13-inch pizza, a 9×13 baking pan, or a 4 lb chicken. Most owners use it as a primary oven for 1–2 person households, reserving the full oven only for large roasts or holiday cooking.
    • The slow cook and proof functions extend its utility well beyond an oven — it maintains temperatures as low as 80°F for bread proofing, and the slow cook mode has a consistent enough temperature to replace a dedicated slow cooker.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • This unit requires at least 4 inches of clearance on all sides and above — significantly more counter space than the footprint suggests. The exterior gets very hot during use; the 18.5-inch width needs additional clearance from cabinets and walls.
    • At $449 CAD, it costs more than most full-size ranges' air fry accessories and significantly more than standalone air fryers. The price is justified by versatility and build quality, but it's a real investment for a countertop appliance.
    • Baking results depend on rack position and pan colour — dark pans absorb more heat and can produce dark bottoms on cookies and cakes. The included rack positions aren't labeled intuitively, and first-time users frequently need to experiment before nailing consistent bakes.
    • The exterior — including the top and door — gets hot enough to cause burns during high-temperature cooking. The handle stays cool, but resting a hand on the top while checking food or reaching over it is a real burn risk.
