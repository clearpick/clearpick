    ✅ What Works

    • 6L tank with 36-hour runtime dramatically reduces refilling frequency — at the lowest cool mist setting, the LV600HH runs 36 hours before needing a refill. For bedroom use, this means once every 1-2 days rather than every few hours like smaller units.
    • Built-in hygrometer with auto mode maintains target humidity — set the desired humidity (e.g., 45%) and the auto mode cycles the humidifier on and off to maintain that level. This prevents over-humidification that can promote mold growth.
    • Warm mist mode warms the bedroom air slightly in addition to adding moisture — in Canadian winters where rooms can feel uncomfortably dry and cool, the warm mist option adds comfort. In summer, cool mist avoids adding heat.
    • Sleep mode at 25 dBA is quiet enough for light sleepers — the lowest setting operates nearly as quietly as the Levoit Core 300 air purifier, enabling continuous overnight operation without disruption.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Ultrasonic technology produces white mineral dust with hard tap water — the LV600HH uses ultrasonic vibration, which aerosolizes minerals from tap water along with the water droplets. Use distilled or demineralized water to prevent white powder depositing on furniture. The Honeywell HCM350 evaporative humidifier avoids this issue.
    • Warm mist uses significantly more energy — the heating element for warm mist operation increases power consumption from ~30W (cool mist) to ~200W. Factor this into electricity cost calculations for continuous use.
    • Requires thorough cleaning every 3 days — ultrasonic transducers develop mineral scale and the water tank can grow bacteria or mold if cleaning is skipped. A weekly vinegar soak plus 3-day rinse is the recommended maintenance routine.
    • The aromatherapy tray is for essential oils only — do not add essential oils directly to the water tank, as they can damage the ultrasonic transducer. Only use the separate aromatherapy tray.
