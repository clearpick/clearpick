    ✅ What Works

    • Vortex heat circulation distributes warmth more evenly than directional ceramic heaters — instead of pushing heat in one direction, the MVH's deep-dish design circulates heated air through the room via vortex action. Rooms feel warmer faster because the heat reaches people regardless of where they're sitting.
    • 5-year warranty is the longest in the consumer space heater category — Vornado backs the MVH with 5 full years of warranty coverage, significantly longer than the 1-year warranty on Lasko and most competitors. Long-term owner reviews consistently confirm units running 6-10 years without failure.
    • Auto shutoffs are dual and reliable — both tip-over and overheat shutoffs are standard and trigger quickly. Particularly important for households with children, pets, or areas with limited ventilation.
    • Compact footprint fits on desks, nightstands, and small floor spaces — the 21x18cm base is significantly smaller than tower heaters, allowing placement in tight quarters like under desks, beside nightstands, or in bathroom corners.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • 1500W like all standard 120V heaters — Vornado's advantage is how heat is distributed, not how much power is used. All residential 120V electric heaters share the 1500W ceiling.
    • Small size means smaller heating element area — the MVH heats rooms primarily through air circulation rather than radiant surface area. For very cold rooms or large spaces, it takes longer to reach target temperature than larger units.
    • Fan noise is audible, not silent — the MVH makes fan noise similar to other ceramic fan heaters. Not suitable for silent bedroom overnight use (see De'Longhi HMP1500 for that).
    • Vornado MVH vs 1500 model — the MVH is the compact version; the 1500 is larger with more heat surface area. Both have the 5-year warranty. The MVH suits smaller spaces.
