    ✅ What Works

    • LFP battery at mid-range price is the EB70S's key advantage — most 500–800Wh competitors use NMC chemistry with 500 cycle life; the EB70S uses LFP with 2500+ cycles, lasting years longer.
    • 716Wh is more usable capacity than 500Wh competitors — extra capacity handles a 12V fridge for 18+ hours, phones for days, and a laptop 6–8 times.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • 800W AC output is limiting for some appliances — works for laptops, fans, and lights; won't handle electric kettles, hair dryers, or larger cooking appliances.
    • 3-hour charge is adequate but not fast — better than Jackery Explorer 500 (7.5 hours) but slower than EcoFlow RIVER 2 Pro (70 min).
