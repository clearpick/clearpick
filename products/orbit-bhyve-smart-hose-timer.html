    ✅ What Works

    • WiFi control from anywhere via the B-hyve app means you can start, stop, or adjust watering schedules from your phone while at work or on vacation — particularly valuable for Canadian cottage-season planning when you're away for weeks.
    • Smart weather delays automatically skip scheduled watering on rain days based on local forecast data — a genuine water-saving feature that many owners say pays for the timer in a single summer.
    • Alexa and Google Assistant integration enables voice control: 'Alexa, start watering the garden' works without opening the app. Setup is straightforward if you already have a smart home ecosystem.
    • Installation takes minutes on a standard 3/4" hose thread — no tools, no plumbing. Screw on, pair to the hub, and schedule from the app.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Requires 2.4 GHz WiFi — does not work on 5 GHz networks. Many modern Canadian routers broadcast both bands under the same name, requiring you to split them or force a 2.4 GHz connection during setup.
    • Single zone only — one hose bib, one watering schedule. For properties with multiple hose connections or garden areas needing different schedules, multiple timers must be purchased and managed separately.
    • Battery-powered (4 AA batteries) — not wired. Battery life is approximately 12 months under typical use, but below-average Canadian winters may reduce this if the timer is left installed year-round.
    • Occasional app connectivity drops require re-pairing — a known issue in the B-hyve ecosystem that appears after firmware updates or router changes. Resolution is usually 5–10 minutes of troubleshooting.
