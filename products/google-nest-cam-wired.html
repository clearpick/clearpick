    ✅ What Works

    • On-device AI processing sets the Nest Cam Wired apart — person, animal, and vehicle detection happens on the camera itself rather than in the cloud, reducing latency for alerts and enabling detection even during internet outages. The distinction between a person vs. a package vs. a vehicle in alerts is accurate and reduces notification fatigue.
    • Google Home integration is the deepest in the category — Nest Cam footage shows natively in the Google Home app, feeds into Assistant routines, and works with Nest Hub displays for at-a-glance live view. If you're already in the Google ecosystem, this camera slots in more seamlessly than any competitor.
    • Night Sight color night vision leverages computational photography (similar to Pixel Night Sight) to produce color images in conditions where most cameras show grainy black-and-white infrared. Porch lights and street lamps provide enough ambient light for genuinely useful color footage.
    • The 10-foot USB-C cable provides meaningful placement flexibility — more than the 6-foot cables shipped with most competing cameras, allowing ceiling mounting or positioning across a room without an extension cable.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Free storage is extremely limited — just 3 hours of event history without a Google Home subscription. Meaningful storage requires Google Home Aware at $8/month (or bundled with Google One), which is among the more expensive subscription models in the category.
    • No microSD card slot means local storage is not an option. Nest Cam is cloud-dependent for any clip retention beyond the brief free event history. For buyers who want subscription-free storage, this camera is not a good fit.
    • Alexa integration is functional but limited compared to native Google Home use — you can pull up a live view on Alexa displays, but detection events and routines don't cross-integrate with Alexa the way they do within Google Home.
    • HDR video is good but the 1080p resolution means it doesn't offer the sharpness of higher-resolution competitors for identifying fine details at distance. For a camera at $129 CAD, 2K or better would be expected from newer entrants.
