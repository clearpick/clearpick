    ✅ What Works

    • Solid spruce top with scalloped X-bracing is the tonal foundation — the solid top and scalloped bracing (where mass is removed from the braces to allow freer top vibration) combine to produce more resonance and dynamics than laminate tops or non-scalloped bracing at this price point.
    • Yamaha's quality control is the most consistent in the entry acoustic category — Yamaha has been manufacturing guitars for 60+ years and applies industrial quality standards to every price tier. The FG800 has the most consistent factory setup and build quality among competing guitars in its price range.
    • Guitar teachers and music stores have recommended the FG800 for 20+ years — the FG series has earned its beginner-standard recommendation through decades of reliable performance. Students who need teacher confidence in their instrument will find universal familiarity with the FG800.
    • Scalloped X-bracing improves projection and dynamic range — by removing material from the bracing pattern, Yamaha allows the spruce top to move more freely, improving the acoustic projection and sensitivity to picking dynamics.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • Nato back and sides is laminate, not solid — nato (Eastern Mahogany) back and sides are laminate construction. Functionally durable and humidity-stable, but tonally less open than solid wood. Only the top is solid wood.
    • The Fender CD-60S is a direct competitor at the same price — both the FG800 and CD-60S are solid-top entry acoustics at similar prices. Key differences: Yamaha has slightly better reputation for quality control consistency; Fender has brand name recognition for future resale. Either is an excellent choice.
    • Factory action may need setup — while Yamaha's factory setup quality is above average, Canadian humidity and shipping conditions mean action may benefit from a professional adjustment after purchase.
    • Yamaha's support network is extensive in Canada — Yamaha has authorized service centers in every major Canadian city, making warranty claims, setup service, and repairs straightforward.
