    ✅ What Works

    • The six-stage configuration allows this chair to grow from infant feeding through a youth chair at the dining table—eliminating the separate booster seat and youth chair purchases that add up significantly over the years
    • Two dishwasher-safe trays (outer tray that stays on chair + removable inner tray that fits in dishwasher top rack) is the most practical tray design available at this price—parents with messy eaters cite this setup as a daily convenience
    • The machine washable seat pad can be laundered regularly without deteriorating—a critical feature in a category where food staining is daily and cheap pad alternatives crack or fade after 10 washes
    • Graco's build quality and parts availability mean broken buckles or worn straps can be replaced without buying a new chair—rare but meaningful for a product expected to last 5–7 years

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • In all stages, the chair takes up full dining-table-chair footprint rather than compressing to a compact size; kitchen storage when not actively in use requires dedicated floor space
    • Transitioning between stages requires time—removing the tray, adjusting the harness, reconfiguring the seating insert—plan 15–20 minutes the first time you convert it between configurations
    • The seat height when in high chair mode is fixed; some dining tables are taller than standard (36 inches) and the Blossom's tray height may not align perfectly with custom or bar-height tables

    Common complaints

    Buckle can be stiff and hard to release

    With food residue in the buckle mechanism (daily reality with a toddler), the crotch buckle can become difficult to press. Regular cleaning with a damp cloth in the buckle cavity solves this but requires attention.

    Source: Amazon.ca reviewer

    Seat pad stains even after washing

    While the pad is machine washable, dark food stains (berries, tomato sauce) can set into the fabric permanently even with immediate attention. Light-colored pad options look notably worse over time than darker colorways.

    Source: Reddit r/Mommit

    Stage conversions take time

    Reconfiguring from infant high chair to toddler booster involves removing multiple components and references to the manual. Parents who expected a 2-minute conversion were surprised by the complexity.

    Source: Amazon.ca reviewer
