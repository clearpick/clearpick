    ✅ What Works

    • The texture produced from a properly frozen 24-hour base is consistently praised — owners describe results as genuinely creamy, not icy or grainy, even with dairy-free and low-sugar bases that typically produce inferior results in other machines.
    • Ingredient control is a recurring positive: owners on keto, diabetic, or dairy-free diets report the machine unlocks frozen desserts they otherwise couldn't eat commercially — this is the most-cited reason for purchase in review threads.
    • The XL Deluxe tubs (24 oz vs. the original 16 oz) are consistently cited as a meaningful upgrade over the base model — family-sized portions without double batching.
    • Versatility across 11 programs is praised by owners who use it beyond ice cream: slushies, creamiccinos, frozen yogurt, and Italian ice all draw repeat mentions as genuine daily-use features.

    ⚠️ Worth Knowing

    • The 24-hour freeze requirement is non-negotiable — this is not an impulse dessert machine, and owners who don't read this upfront report frustration on first use. Plan a day ahead.
    • Noise level during processing is consistently described as very loud — comparable to a blender running on full — for a 2–3 minute cycle. A recurring note in apartment owner reviews.
    • The outer lid does not fully disassemble for cleaning, requiring a small brush to reach interior crevices — a minor but recurring complaint in long-term ownership reviews.
