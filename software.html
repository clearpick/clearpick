    Security and Productivity Software Worth Paying For

    Antivirus and productivity software is one of the easiest categories to overspend in — most people pay for features they'll never use. The right choice depends on how many devices you need to cover, whether you want bundled extras like a VPN or password manager, and for antivirus, some genuinely important considerations around where the company is based.

    ClearPick's software scores combine independent lab testing from AV-TEST and AV-Comparatives with real user feedback and reviews from Tom's Guide and SafetyDetectives. For Canadian buyers we also flag anything relevant to government guidance or data sovereignty.
