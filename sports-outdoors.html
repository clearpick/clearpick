    The best sports and outdoor gear earns its price through longevity and performance under real conditions — not lab specifications. A pair of HOKA Clifton 9s will protect your joints through a marathon training cycle in a way that budget shoes simply can't. The challenge is identifying which premium features are functional upgrades and which are marketing.
