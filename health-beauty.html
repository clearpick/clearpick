    Health and Beauty Devices Worth Buying in Canada

    The best personal care devices share one trait: they save time or deliver results that manual alternatives can't match. A sonic toothbrush outperforms manual brushing regardless of technique. But the category also has more premium pricing than almost any other — the gap between a $49 and a $699 hair dryer is real, and worth understanding before you spend.
