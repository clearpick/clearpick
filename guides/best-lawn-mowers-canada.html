What to Look For

Lot size vs. battery capacity

The standard Canadian suburban lot (50x120 ft total area, roughly 4,000 sq ft of mowable lawn) falls comfortably within EGO's single-charge range. Lots over 10,000 sq ft of mowable lawn should factor battery capacity carefully — the EGO 7.5Ah battery handles up to roughly 1 acre per charge in owner data. For larger properties, robot mowers often make more economic sense when labour cost is factored in.

Gas vs. electric in Canada

Gas mowers require winterization — draining fuel or adding stabilizer — before storage. Skipping this is the single most common cause of spring startup failures in Canadian owner reviews. Electric mowers skip this entirely. However, gas engines still outperform electric in very tall or wet grass — owners who mow infrequently (every 10–14 days during peak growth) consistently report gas handling difficult cuts better than cordless at equivalent prices.

Robot mower seasonal considerations

Robot mowers should be brought indoors for Canadian winters — storing lithium batteries in sub-freezing temperatures degrades capacity over time. Most owners with garages manage this without issue. Canadian spring startup (April to May depending on region) may require re-calibration if frost heave has shifted boundary wire stakes or GPS reference points.

Self-propel for Canadian terrain

Slopes over 15–20% grade make self-propel necessary, not optional. Many Canadian properties — particularly in BC, the Niagara Escarpment area, and Atlantic provinces — have significant grade changes. The Honda HRX217VKA and EGO LM2156SP both handle 20% slopes reliably per owner data. The Mammotion LUBA 3 AWD handles up to 45% slopes, the highest in this guide.

Service and parts availability

Honda and RYOBI have broad service networks across Canadian cities and many rural areas. EGO's service network is growing but remains concentrated in major urban centres. For robot mowers, Husqvarna's dealer network is the most extensive in Canada — a meaningful advantage for buyers in smaller centres who cannot easily ship heavy equipment for warranty work.

FAQ

Is the EGO mower available at Canadian Tire?

EGO Power+ is sold primarily through Home Depot Canada, Home Hardware, and Amazon.ca. Canadian Tire carries RYOBI and Greenworks more prominently. In-store demos are most common at Home Depot locations for EGO.

How do robot mowers handle Canadian spring conditions?

Spring conditions — wet soil, matted grass from snow mould, uneven terrain after frost heave — are challenging for all robot mowers. Most owners delay robot mower activation until late May when the lawn has dried and been manually raked or first-cut. A single manual cut to clear debris before activating the robot is the standard recommendation in r/lawncare Canada discussions.

Do robot mowers work with Canadian wireless carriers?

GPS-based robot mowers like LUBA 3 and Segway Navimow use RTK GPS satellites and do not depend on Canadian cellular carriers. App connectivity uses home WiFi. There are no carrier compatibility issues specific to Canada.

What is the warranty situation for EGO in Canada?

EGO offers a 5-year tool warranty and 3-year battery warranty when registered in Canada — comparable to Honda's 3-year residential engine warranty. Husqvarna robot mowers carry a 2-year warranty through authorized Canadian dealers.
