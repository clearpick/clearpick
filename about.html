    About ClearPick

    An independent review site built by someone who got tired of AI tool recommendations that were clearly just paid placements.

    Why ClearPick Exists

    The AI tools space moves fast — new tools launch every week, pricing changes constantly, and most "best of" lists online are written by people who never actually used the tools they're recommending.

    ClearPick exists to cut through that noise. Every tool on this site gets real, hands-on testing. We use them for actual work — writing articles, generating images, coding projects, editing videos — before we rank them. If a tool doesn't hold up in practice, it doesn't rank highly here, regardless of how much commission it pays.

    How We Test Tools

    Every tool is scored across five dimensions:

    Output Quality

    Does it actually produce good results in real-world use cases?

    Ease of Use

    Can a non-technical person get value from it quickly?

    Value for Money

    Is the pricing fair relative to what you get?

    Feature Depth

    Does it do one thing well or offer a genuinely useful range of capabilities?

    Reliability

    Does it consistently deliver, or does it break down under real workloads?

    Our Affiliate Policy

    ClearPick earns commissions through affiliate links — when you click a link on this site and purchase a tool, we may earn a percentage at no extra cost to you. This is how we keep the site running.

    However, commission rates never influence rankings. A tool that pays us 5% but is genuinely the best option will always rank above a tool that pays 30% but underdelivers. We'd rather recommend something great and earn less than recommend something mediocre and earn more — that's the only way to build a site people trust.

    All affiliate links on ClearPick are clearly marked with rel="sponsored" in the HTML and disclosed in our footer.

    Updates & Accuracy

    Rankings are reviewed and updated monthly. AI tools change rapidly — pricing updates, new features ship, quality shifts — so we re-test regularly to make sure our recommendations stay accurate. The last review date is shown on every category page.
