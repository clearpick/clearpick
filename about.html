    About ClearPick

    ClearPick exists because I got tired of scraping through reviews.

    When I want to buy something, even something small, I want to know what people who actually own it think. Not influencers. Not paid reviewers. Real buyers, in unfiltered places like Reddit and Amazon's verified-purchase reviews. Finding the signal takes hours, and most "review sites" just rephrase manufacturer marketing copy and call it editorial.

    ClearPick is what I wished existed. One review per product, with thousands of opinions behind it. No sponsored rankings. No AI-generated filler. No padding the source list to look credible.

    About the reviewer

    My name is Nathan P. I'm a Canadian veteran. I served nine years in the Canadian Armed Forces. I'm the person who used to spend an hour comparing product specs across six tabs before buying a $50 gadget, and decided to build the tool I wished existed instead.

    ClearPick is a one-person operation. I do all the research and stand behind every score on the site. If something's wrong, I want to know.

    What you won't find on ClearPick

    • Sponsored rankings or paid placements
    • AI-generated review filler (AI tools assist with research, but conclusions and scores are mine)
    • Fake source counts or padded source lists
    • Hidden negatives — every product page includes the real complaints
