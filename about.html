    About ClearPick

    ClearPick is a Canadian buyer's guide built on one principle: real buyers are more honest than anyone paid to be.

    What We Do

    For every product on ClearPick, we spend hours in the places where real owners actually talk: Reddit threads, Amazon review sections, owner forums, and long-term user communities. We read the good reviews and the complaints. We look for patterns — what do people love six months in? What breaks? What did the marketing leave out?

    Then we synthesize it into one page: a score, a verdict, and the most important things to know before you buy. No fluff, no affiliate-incentivized hype.

    Our Editorial Process

    Every ClearPick page is built from the same research process:

    • Reddit first. Product-specific subreddits and buying advice communities are where owners speak candidly — no brand relationship, no review copies, no incentive to be positive. This is our primary source.
    • Amazon reviews, filtered. We focus on 2–4 star reviews, which is where honest trade-offs live. 5-star reviews are often promotional; 1-star reviews are often shipping complaints. The middle is where the truth is.
    • Expert publications for technical context. RTINGS, Wirecutter, Consumer Reports, and category specialists provide specs, testing methodology, and technical benchmarks. We use them for accuracy, not for voice.
    • Long-term ownership reports. We specifically seek out feedback from people who have owned a product for 6–24 months. First-week impressions and long-term reality are often very different.

    Scores reflect aggregate owner sentiment, not a single reviewer's opinion. A product can have excellent professional reviews and a mediocre ClearPick score if real buyers consistently report problems. The inverse is also true.

    For the full breakdown of how scores are calculated, see our methodology page.

    Our Commitment

    • No sponsored rankings. Brands cannot pay to improve their score or placement on ClearPick. Our scores are editorially independent.
    • We show the complaints. Every product page includes sourced negative feedback. A page without real complaints isn't honest — it's marketing.
    • We're Canadian. Prices are in CAD, sourced from Amazon.ca. We don't convert US prices and pretend they apply here.
    • Affiliate disclosure. ClearPick uses affiliate links. When you buy through a link, we may earn a small commission at no cost to you. This never influences our scores — a product can score 6.2 and we'll still link to it accurately.
