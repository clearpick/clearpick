    Building a Smart Home That Works With What You Have

    The biggest smart home mistake is buying devices from three different ecosystems and then fighting compatibility for years. Before buying anything, it's worth knowing whether your household leans toward Amazon Alexa, Google Home, or Apple HomeKit — that single decision affects which doorbells, thermostats, and speakers will actually work together.

    Our scores are built from Amazon.ca feedback, smart home community discussion, and testing from Tom's Guide and CNET. For thermostats specifically, we highlight documented energy savings and note which Canadian utility companies offer rebates.

    Each review notes which ecosystem a product fits best, what's included free versus subscription-only, and how it performs in Canadian conditions like long winters and older-home WiFi setups.
